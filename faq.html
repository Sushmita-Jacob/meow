Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is Kitty Adoption?

Learn more about Kitty Adoption on our About Us page.

How was Kitty Adoption founded?

Kitty Adoption started in 2125 by a team of teenage cat lovers. After the CEO took care of two stray cats at the young age of 15, him and his friends wanted to impact the greater community and potentially the whole world. The group partnered with external organizations for support.

How can I help?

Support Kitty Adoption by donating to our GoFundMe! Additionally, the adoption center is open on Saturdays from 9 am to 5 pm for volunteers.
